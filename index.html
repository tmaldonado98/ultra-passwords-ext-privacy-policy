Privacy Policy

This extension is committed to protecting your privacy. We want you to understand clearly how our product handles (or does not handle) your data.

1. Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing data, or user-generated content. No data is gathered from your device.

2. Data Usage

Because no data is collected, this extension does not use your data for any purpose. All functionality occurs locally within your browser.

3. Data Sharing

This extension does not share any information with third parties, advertisers, analytics providers, or any other external services.

4. Third Parties

No user data is shared with or processed by any external parties. The extension operates independently without external data connections.

If in the future this extension introduces features that involve the collection or use of personal data, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be notified of the changes.
